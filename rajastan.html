About Rajasthan

Rajasthan, India's largest state, is known for its majestic forts, palaces, deserts, and vibrant traditions. The royal charm of Jaipur, the golden dunes of Jaisalmer, and the lakes of Udaipur reflect its timeless beauty. Folk dances like Ghoomar and Kalbeliya bring alive the colorful culture and spirit of Rajasthan.
